Bikaner (Rajasthan)

CHHOTU MOTU JOSHI
What to Try: Bikaneri bhujia, daal baati churma, and sweets like rasgulla.

Mysore (Karnataka)

R R R RESTURANT
What to Try: Ragi rotti, Mysore masala dosa, and traditional biryani.

Rishikesh (Uttarakhand)

CHOTIWALA RESTURANT
What to Try: Rajma chawal, aloo paratha, and chole bhature.

Vishakhapatnam (Andhra Pradesh)

DHARANI RESTURANT
What to Try: Andhra-style biryani, seafood, and gongura pachadi (sour leaf chutney).

Jaisalmer (Rajasthan)

TTHE TRIO
What to Try: Laal maas, gatte ki sabzi, dal baati churma.

Khajuraho (Madhya Pradesh)

THE RAJA CAFE
What to Try: Tandoori chicken, samosas, and Indian curries.
